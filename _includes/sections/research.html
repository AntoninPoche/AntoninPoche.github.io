PhD goal

The goal of my PhD is to build an interpretability agent (conversational explainability) for language models.

Current issue

However, I find that the quality and consistency of explanations are lacking, in particular with concept-based explanations.

Current work

Which is why I currently work on improving such explanations.

ConSim

Notably, the first publication of my PhD, ConSim (Poché et al. ACL 2025), aims at evaluating the usefulness of concept-based explanations in NLP.

Future directions

The future directions I would like to explore are:

  • Improving the interpretation of concept-based explanations.
  • Building contrastive concept-based explanations.
  • Example-based explanations for language models.
